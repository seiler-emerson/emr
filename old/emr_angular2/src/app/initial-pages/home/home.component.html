About

History

Thinking about health institutions that were unable to adapt to digitalization, EMR was born, a simple software with low complexity to facilitate the use of electronic medical records.

Purpose

Bring digital transformation to small, non-digitized institutions.

Tecnology

Solve pain in the area through technology.

Solution

The EMR is an electronic medical record system that aims to deal with the management of small clinics and medical care offices. Focused on the management of electronic medical records, it is able to store and manage information related to patients during the service cycles. With the history, it is possible for the health professional to know the personalities of the patient's framework and thus make better decisions. Another important point is related to the issue of printed documentation, thus eliminating the possibility of errors caused by handwriting interpretation problems.

Functionalities

Appointment

The appointments section is the part where the patient appointments data is listed, created, edited and deleted. Here you can find information regarding each appointment performed.

Patient

The patient section is the part where patient data is listed, created, edited and deleted. Here you can find personal information, in addition to the total list of patients.

Doctor

The doctors section is the part where the doctors data is listed, created, edited and deleted. Here you can find personal information, in addition to the total list of doctors.

User

The users section is the part where user data is listed, created, edited and deleted. Here you can find your records information.
